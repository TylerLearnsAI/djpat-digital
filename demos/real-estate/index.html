Neighborhood Guide

Nashville's neighborhoods each have their own character and charm. Here are the areas where I specialize — and where my clients love to call home.

Belle Meade

Avg. $1.8M Top Schools

Nashville's most prestigious address. Estate homes on sprawling lots, tree-lined streets, and proximity to Percy Warner Park. Home to Belle Meade Country Club and top-rated private schools.

The Gulch

Avg. $680K Walk Score: 92

Nashville's trendiest urban neighborhood. Luxury condos, rooftop bars, James Beard-nominated restaurants, and boutique shopping. Perfect for professionals who want walkable city living.

East Nashville

Avg. $520K Fastest Growing

The creative heart of Nashville. Eclectic mix of renovated bungalows, new construction, and local-owned businesses. Five Points, Lockeland Springs, and Shelby Park offer vibrant community living.

12 South

Avg. $890K Top Dining

Where Nashville charm meets boutique culture. The iconic Draper James storefront, artisan coffee shops, and some of the city's best restaurants — all on a walkable strip surrounded by updated Craftsman homes.
